About Us




The Amazon

Amazon.com, Inc. is an American multinational technology company focusing on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos, it started as an online bookstore and has since grown into a global e-commerce giant and a major player in cloud computing with its Amazon Web Services (AWS). Amazon also offers a wide range of other services and products, including streaming media, consumer electronics, and various other retail goods.

E-commerce: Amazon operates the world's largest online marketplace, offering a vast selection of products from books and electronics to apparel and home goods.

Cloud Computing: Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a leading provider of cloud computing services, including computing power, storage, databases, analytics, and more.

Digital Streaming: Amazon offers streaming services through Amazon Prime Video and Amazon Music, as well as the live streaming platform Twitch.

Consumer Electronics: Amazon designs and produces a range of consumer electronics, including Kindle e-readers, Echo devices, Fire tablets, and Fire TVs.

Jeffrey Preston Bezos (/ˈbeɪzoʊs/ BAY-zohss; né Jorgensen; born January 12, 1964) is an American businessman best known as the founder, executive chairman, and former president and CEO of Amazon, the world's largest e-commerce and cloud computing company.


The CEO Of Amazon

Andy Jassy

Andy Jassy is President and CEO of Amazon.com and also serves on the Board of Directors. He founded and led Amazon Web Services (AWS) from its inception and served as its CEO from April 2016 until July 2021.
